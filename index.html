Captcha!

Captcha! is a game to test your ability to differentiate between image subjects.

You can access the options menu by clicking Game Options.

To start playing type two words separated by a comma and click Start Game.

Right answers add a point to the score while wrong answers subtract a point.

Click the Next Page button to get to the next round of images.

The game will end when the timer runs out.
